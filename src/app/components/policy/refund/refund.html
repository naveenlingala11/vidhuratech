Refund Policy

Please read our refund terms carefully before enrolling in any program

At Vidhura Tech, we strive to deliver high-quality training programs and career support services. This Refund Policy outlines the terms under which refunds may or may not be granted.

⚠️ By enrolling in any course, you agree to this Refund Policy.

1. General Refund Policy

All payments made for training programs, mentorship sessions, or any services offered by Vidhura Tech are considered final.

  • Fees once paid are generally non-refundable
  • Access to course materials confirms service initiation
  • Refunds are not applicable for completed or ongoing courses

We strongly recommend reviewing course details before enrolling.

2. Eligibility for Refund (Exceptional Cases)

Refunds may be considered only under exceptional circumstances and are subject to management approval.

  • Duplicate payment made by mistake
  • Technical issues preventing course access
  • Course cancellation by Vidhura Tech
  • Valid medical or personal emergencies (with proof)

Submission of a refund request does not guarantee approval.

3. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will NOT be provided in the following cases:

  • Change of mind after enrollment
  • Lack of time or inability to attend sessions
  • Dissatisfaction without valid reason
  • Partial course completion
  • Failure to complete assignments or tasks

4. Refund Request Process

To request a refund, users must contact our support team within the allowed timeframe.

  • Submit request via email with payment details
  • Provide valid reason and supporting documents
  • Wait for review and approval

Our team will evaluate the request and respond within a reasonable timeframe.

5. Refund Processing Time

If a refund is approved, the amount will be processed within 5–10 business days.

  • Refunds will be issued via the original payment method
  • Processing time may vary depending on bank/payment gateway
  • Transaction fees may be deducted if applicable

6. Course Cancellation by Us

In rare situations where a course is cancelled by Vidhura Tech:

  • Users will be eligible for a full refund OR
  • Option to enroll in another course

We will notify users in advance regarding such cancellations.
